# Broadcast Ops - Volunteer Guide

## What is this?

A mobile web app that replaces the WhatsApp chaos. It shows interns where you are, lets you claim escort jobs, and keeps everyone on the same page — all from your phone browser.

**URL:** https://fifa-broadcast-ops-production.up.railway.app/

No login. No download. Just open the link.

---

## Install on Your Phone (Recommended)

Installing the app on your home screen gives you push notifications — you'll get alerted even if your phone is locked.

**iPhone (iOS):**
1. Open the URL above in **Safari**
2. Tap the **Share button** (square with arrow, bottom of screen)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **Add**
5. Open the app from your home screen
6. Tap the 🔕 bell icon in the header → allow notifications

**Android:**
1. Open the URL in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu → **"Add to Home Screen"** (or you may see an install banner)
3. Tap the 🔕 bell icon → allow notifications

If you skip this step, the app still works in your browser — you just won't get notifications when your phone is locked.

---

## Getting Started (30 seconds)

1. Open the link on your phone
2. Tap **Volunteer** at the top
3. Go to the **My Status** tab
4. Tap your name from the grid
5. Tap **Check In**

That's it — you're on the board and interns can see you.

---

## Updating Your Location

When you move somewhere, tap **My Status** → tap the new location button.

Locations include: Media Center, BIO, Commentary, Press Box (Red), Press Box (Blue), Pitch, Announce Platform, etc.

**Do this every time you move.** It's one tap and it saves everyone from asking "where are you?" over WhatsApp.

---

## Claiming an Escort

When an intern posts an escort request:

1. Go to the **Escorts** tab
2. You'll see pending requests like: **"SVT: Media Center → Announce Platform"**
3. Tap **Claim This Escort**
4. Your status automatically changes to "Escorting" and location to "En Route"
5. Walk the Media Partner to their destination
6. Tap **Mark Delivered** when done — your location updates to the destination

---

## Going on Break

1. **My Status** tab → tap **Go On Break**
2. Interns will see you're unavailable
3. When you're back, tap **Back from Break**

**Please take breaks when prompted** — it helps interns plan coverage.

---

## Checking Out

When your shift ends: **My Status** → **Check Out**. You disappear from the active board.

---

## Checking Alerts

The **Alerts** tab shows messages from interns/staff — things like "Media Center needs coverage" or "Please take dinner in pairs." Check it periodically.

---

## Example Scenarios

### Scenario: You just arrived for a 3-9 shift

1. Open the app → Tap your name → Check In
2. Check the **Board** tab — see where coverage is thin
3. Check **Escorts** — any pending requests you can grab?
4. Head to where you're needed and update your location

### Scenario: An intern asks you to escort someone

1. You'll see it pop up in the **Escorts** tab
2. Tap **Claim** → go to the Media Center (or wherever)
3. Walk the Media Partner to their destination
4. Tap **Mark Delivered**
5. Check the Board — stay where you are or head back as needed

### Scenario: You need dinner

1. Check with the board — make sure your location won't be empty
2. Tap **Go On Break**
3. Eat (30 min)
4. Tap **Back from Break** and update your location

---

## Tips

- **Keep your location current** — it's the single most helpful thing you can do
- **Check Escorts often** — new ones appear in real-time
- **Don't wait for WhatsApp** — if you see a pending escort, just claim it
- **The Board is your friend** — before asking "where should I go?", check which locations are red/understaffed
