# Broadcast Ops - Intern & Staff Guide

## What is this?

A real-time coordination board for managing volunteers during match days. Instead of juggling WhatsApp threads to figure out who's where and who's free, everything is visible at a glance on one screen.

**URL:** _(will be provided)_

No login. No accounts. Works on any phone browser.

---

## Getting Started

1. Open the link on your phone
2. Tap **Intern / Staff** at the top
3. Tap your name (or type it if you're new)

You're in. Your name will show on any escort requests or announcements you create.

---

## The Board Tab (Your Home Screen)

Shows every checked-in volunteer grouped by location:

- **Green count** = location is staffed at or above minimum
- **Red count** = understaffed — needs someone sent there
- **On Break** section shows who's unavailable

Use this to answer the question: _"Who's where right now?"_

---

## Creating Escort Requests

When a Media Partner needs to go somewhere:

1. Go to **Escorts** tab
2. Tap **+ New Escort Request**
3. Fill in:
   - **Media Partner** name (e.g. "SVT", "CCTV", "ITV Sport")
   - **From** (e.g. Media Center)
   - **To** (e.g. BIO, Announce Platform, Commentary)
4. Tap **Create Escort Request**

Volunteers see it immediately and can claim it with one tap. You'll see who claimed it and when they mark it delivered.

No more "can someone take X to Y?" in the group chat.

---

## Managing Coverage

The **Coverage** tab shows headcount at every location. You can set minimum requirements:

- Media Center: **Min 2** (always need two people there)
- BIO: **Min 1**
- Others: set as needed per game

When a location drops below its minimum, it shows red on the Board. Volunteers can see this too.

---

## Broadcasting Announcements

Need to tell all volunteers something?

1. **Alerts** tab → type your message
2. Tap **Broadcast to All**

Examples:
- "Please take your lunches when prompted — it helps us plan"
- "Water delivery at halftime — Dan report to CCR"
- "Shift change at 6pm — check the Board for new assignments"

You can dismiss old announcements when they're no longer relevant.

---

## Managing People & Locations

On the **Board** tab, you'll see management buttons:

### Manage Volunteers
- Rename generic slots ("Volunteer 11" → "Sarah") when new people join the pool
- Pool is always 20 slots — rename as needed between games

### Manage Locations
- Add new locations (e.g. "MP Cabin 6") as they come up
- Rename existing ones if terminology changes

### Manage Interns/Staff
- Add/rename intern and staff names so the picker stays current

## Example Scenarios

### Scenario: Pre-game setup (2 hours before kickoff)

1. Open the Board — see who's checked in
2. Ensure Media Center has 2 people (set coverage rule if not already)
3. Post an announcement: "Welcome! Please check in and update your location."
4. As Media Partners arrive, create escort requests as needed

### Scenario: Multiple escorts needed at once

1. Create escort requests for each MP (they queue up)
2. Volunteers claim them independently — no need to assign manually
3. Watch the Escorts tab — you'll see who's handling what
4. If no one claims after a minute, check the Board for free volunteers and ask them directly

### Scenario: Halftime coverage rotation

1. Check the Board — who's been at their post longest?
2. Post announcement: "Halftime — rotate breaks. Two at a time."
3. Volunteers tap "Go On Break" — you see coverage update in real-time
4. If a location goes red, you know immediately

### Scenario: Volunteer asks "where should I go?"

Instead of figuring it out over WhatsApp:
1. Look at the Board — find the red/understaffed location
2. Tell them: "Media Center needs you" — or they can see it themselves

---

## Tips

- **Check the Board before texting WhatsApp** — the answer is usually already there
- **Let volunteers self-serve** — if they can see the Board, they know where help is needed
- **Use Escorts for any movement** — it creates a record and updates locations automatically
- **Coverage rules are your early warning system** — set them for critical positions
- **Announcements > group texts** — they persist and new volunteers see them when they check in

---

## Switching Back

Tap **"switch"** next to your name in the header to change who you are, or tap **Volunteer** to see the volunteer view if you need to troubleshoot for someone.
